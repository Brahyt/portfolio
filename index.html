Myself

Hello, my name is Daniel Bright. My spirit tree is the cedar, I know... best tree ever, right? Poets and writers have pulled inspiration from this mighty tree for its strength and its eternity. It symbolizes my eternal quest for knowledge and my persistence and patience in understanding all around me. I enjoy coding because of the expanding source of information and my need to grow and make a difference. I have been working as a chef at some of the hottest restaurants in San Diego but have always had a passion to work with code. I have recently joined thinkful bootcamp to make the transition into becoming a junior developer.

Like a good cedar, I am flexible and persistent. I look forward to working with you and your team of great programmers! I know I would make a great choice to help you build whatever you wish to.
