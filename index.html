Who am I?

Hello, my name is Daniel Bright. My spirit tree is cedar, I know right? Poets and writers have pulled inspiration from this mighty tree for its strength and its eternity. It symbolizes my eternal quest for knowledge and my persistence and patience in understanding all around me. I enjoy coding because of the expanding source of information and my need to grow and make my mark. I have been working as a chef at some of the hottest restaurants in San Diego but have always had a passion to work with code. I have recently joined thinkful bootcamp to make the transition into becoming a junior developer.

Like a good cedar, I am flexible but persistent. I know I would make a great choice to help you build whatever you wish to.
